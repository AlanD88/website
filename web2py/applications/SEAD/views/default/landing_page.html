S.E.A.D.S

Smart Energy Analytics Disaggregation Systems

Cross Platform Support

Our current product SEADS uses advanced machine learning algorithms to notify the customers when and how their appliances need maintenance to reduce costly maintenance calls or equipment failures that can lead to loss of product and customer dissatisfaction. SEADS also informs customers how to operate their appliances to reduce electricity costs and carbon emissions.

Intelligent Monitoring

Inside SEADS lies an intelligent agent for disaggregation and signature monitoring of appliances. The data is sampled and analyzed at a high resolution sampling rate which allows our system to monitor the infinitesimal changes in the appliances behavior which can predict when and how an appliance, such as an air conditioner and refrigerator, is starting to fail. SEADS notifies the user or service reps when the device diverges from its regular condition so that they can reduce operating inefficiencies and repair the appliance before it fails.

Cost analysis

With SEADS smart monitoring you will be able to monitor every small detail of your energy consumption leading you to make smarter decisions about your appliance use and save money.
